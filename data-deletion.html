Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: July 15, 2026

At OutplorerApp, we respect your privacy and your right to control your data. This page explains how you can request deletion of your data from our service.

What Data We Can Delete

OutplorerApp only accesses publicly available social media data through official Meta APIs (including the Instagram Graph API and Facebook Graph API). We do not store passwords, private messages, direct messages, or other non-public information.

However, if you believe that OutplorerApp has collected or stored any of your data and you would like it removed, you may request deletion using the methods below.

How to Request Data Deletion

Method 1: Email Request

Send an email to {{tob@zeapon.com}} with the subject line "Data Deletion Request".

Please include the following information in your request:

  • Your Instagram or Facebook username (if applicable).
  • The specific data you would like deleted, or state "Delete all data associated with my account".
  • A brief description of your request.
  • Contact information for confirmation purposes.

Method 2: Revoke Access via Meta

You can also revoke OutplorerApp's access to your Instagram or Facebook data at any time through your Meta account settings:

  1. Go to Facebook or Instagram settings.
  2. Navigate to Apps and Websites (or Security → Apps and Websites on Instagram).
  3. Find OutplorerApp and select Remove or Revoke Access.

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days of receiving it. Once completed, we will send a confirmation email to the address you provided.

Data Deletion Scope

Upon receiving your request, we will delete:

  • Any cached public social media data associated with your account or username.
  • Your contact information and request history related to the deletion.

Please note that some information may be retained where required by law or for legitimate business purposes, such as fraud prevention or legal compliance, for the minimum period required by applicable law.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion or your privacy rights, please contact us at {{tob@zeapon.com}}.
