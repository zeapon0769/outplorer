Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how OutplorerApp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use our application or website. By using OutplorerApp, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

OutplorerApp is a creator discovery and analytics tool. We access publicly available social media data through official Meta APIs (including the Instagram Graph API and Facebook Graph API) to help users discover and research public creators and hashtags.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Public Instagram data obtained via the official Meta Instagram Graph API, including public usernames, post captions, media URLs, like counts, comment counts, follower counts, and other publicly available profile metrics.
  • Public Facebook Page data obtained via the official Facebook Graph API, including publicly available Page names, engagement metrics, and public posts.
  • Contact information that you voluntarily provide when you contact us, such as your email address, Instagram username, and the content of your request.

We do not collect or store passwords, private messages, direct messages, non-public account data, or any other sensitive personal information.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and improve the OutplorerApp service.
  • To generate aggregated, non-personal creator research and analytics reports.
  • To respond to your inquiries, deletion requests, and support requests.
  • To comply with applicable laws and enforce our Terms of Service.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA) or the United Kingdom, our legal basis for processing personal data is:

  • Legitimate interest in providing creator discovery and analytics services using publicly available data.
  • Your consent, where we rely on it to contact you or process specific requests.
  • Legal obligation, where we are required to process data by law.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With service providers who help us operate the service (e.g., hosting providers) and are contractually bound to protect your data.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect our rights and safety.

6. Data Retention

Public social media data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the requested analytics services. We regularly delete cached public data within 30 days unless a longer retention period is required by law or you request earlier deletion.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.
  • Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

9. International Data Transfers
